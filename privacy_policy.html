Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 6, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Pure Scan – Food Scanner ("we," "our," or "the App") collects, uses, and protects your information.

Introduction

Pure Scan – Food Scanner is owned and operated by Austin Pulliam. If you have any questions about this policy, please contact us at support@purescan.app.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data to provide you with the best possible experience. The types of data we may collect include:

  • Camera Access: We request access to your device's camera solely for the purpose of scanning food labels (barcodes and ingredient lists). All image processing is done locally on your device or temporarily for the scan session. We do not store your photos on our servers.
  • App Usage Data: We collect anonymous data about how you use the app, such as which features you access, crash reports, and performance metrics. This helps us improve the app's stability and functionality.
  • Subscription and Purchase Status: All financial transactions and subscriptions are handled directly by Apple via the App Store. We receive a status update (e.g., "Active" or "Expired") but do not see or store your credit card details or billing address.
  • Device Information: We may collect basic information about your device, such as the operating system version and device model, to ensure compatibility and troubleshoot issues.
Privacy Commitment: We do NOT collect sensitive personal data (such as health records or precise location), and we do NOT sell your personal data to third parties.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used for the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate, and maintain the app's core functionality (scanning food products).
  • To manage your subscriptions and unlock premium features.
  • To detect and fix bugs, crashes, and performance issues.
  • To improve the app based on aggregate usage trends.

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services to help us operate the app. These services process data only as necessary to perform their functions:

  • Apple In-App Purchases: Handles all billing and subscription management.
  • Analytics Services: We may use tools (such as Apple or Firebase) to understand aggregate usage patterns.
  • Paywall and Subscription Services (e.g., Superwall): Helps us manage and display subscription options.

Data Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy. Since most processing happens locally on your device, we do not maintain long-term storage of your personal scanning history on our servers. You may request the deletion of any optional non-purchase data we might hold by contacting support.

User Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access information held about you (if any).
  • Request updates or corrections to your data.
  • Request deletion of your data.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at support@purescan.app.

Children's Privacy

Pure Scan is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page. Your continued use of the app after any changes indicates your acceptance of the new policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or suggestions about our Privacy Policy, do not hesitate to contact us at support@purescan.app.
