Introduction

Pure Scan – Food Scanner (“Pure Scan,” “we,” “us,” or “our”) is owned and operated by Austin Pulliam. We value your privacy and are committed to protecting it. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Pure Scan mobile application.

If you have any questions, you can contact us at support@purescan.app.

Information We Collect

Camera Access

Pure Scan may request access to your device’s camera solely for scanning food labels and ingredient lists. Images are processed locally on your device or temporarily for analysis and are not permanently stored by us.

App Usage Data

We may collect non-personal usage information such as feature interactions, performance data, and crash reports. This data helps us improve app reliability and user experience.

Subscription and Purchase Information

All subscriptions and payments are processed through Apple In-App Purchases. We do not collect or store payment details. We only receive information about whether a subscription is active or inactive.

Device Information

We may collect basic device information such as device type, operating system version, and app version.

What We Do Not Collect

Pure Scan does not collect sensitive personal data such as medical records, precise location data, biometric information, or contact lists. We do not sell personal data to third parties.

How Information Is Used

Information is used to:

  • Provide and maintain app functionality
  • Enable premium features and subscriptions
  • Fix bugs and improve performance

Third-Party Services

Pure Scan may use third-party services such as:

  • Apple In-App Purchases
  • Analytics services (e.g., Apple, Firebase, or similar)
  • Paywall and subscription services (e.g., Superwall)

These services only receive information necessary to perform their functions and are not permitted to use it for other purposes.

Data Retention

We retain information only for as long as necessary to operate and improve the app. Users may request deletion of non-purchase-related data by contacting support.

User Rights

You have the right to access, update, or request deletion of your data. To exercise these rights, contact us at support@purescan.app.

Children’s Privacy

Pure Scan is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
